Overview

PT-ID02 is a reliable ID Card personal tracker that integrates GSM, GPS, GPRS, AGPS, and Optional wifi and LBS technologies to provide accurate real-time tracking. It is a device to track the safety of your loved one. It can be used for children, old citizens, and all with special needs. Its functions include tracking, geo fence, and trace playback, and comes with a mic that supports 2-way communication, voice recording, and sound call back for efficient personal safety. Its compact lightweight design makes it more comfortable to carry with a 1500mAh rechargeable battery for better durable performance. It provides accurate and fast monitoring through APP, Web, or SMS platforms and has an SOS feature in case of emergency. It is best for the personal safety and security.
